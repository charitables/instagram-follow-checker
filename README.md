This script uses undocumented Instagram endpoints.
It is provided for educational purposes only.

- No actions are performed on your account
- No data is stored or transmitted
- You run this manually in your own browser
- Use at your own risk

Instagram may change or block these endpoints at any time.

---

## How to use

1. Open **https://www.instagram.com** in your browser  
2. **Log in** to the Instagram account you want to check  
3. Navigate to **your profile page**
4. Open your browserâ€™s **Developer Tools**
   - Chrome / Edge / Brave: `F12` or `Ctrl + Shift + I`
   - macOS: `Cmd + Option + I`
5. Go to the **Console** tab
6. If your browser shows a warning about pasting code, **type `allow pasting` and press Enter**
7. Open the file **`app.js`** from this repository
8. Copy the entire contents of **`app.js`**
9. Paste the script into the console and press **Enter**
10. Wait while the script runs (this may take a few moments depending on how many accounts you follow)
11. When finished, the console will print a list of usernames that **do not follow you back**
